Imagination of Things is a design fiction studio

Imagination of Things is a creative studio that uses design, fiction, and technology to craft meaningful stories.

We create content and experiences – digital, physical, and beyond – to spark cultural impact. We are storytellers, inventors, and magicians.

We also developed a collaborative design process that uses fiction as a toolset to bring imaginative outcomes to startups, cultural institutions, and companies. Together, we envision alternatives to current challenges and prototype them into tangible directions for innovation.

We’ve disrupted the EU parliament official tour with an AR experience, turned our smart phone into a robot interface for the car, insured sun on your holiday, made strangers compose music in an elevator, and set off an empathy bomb in a city square.

Full portfolio on request.

This website is an experiment that takes the most searched words on Google in your country and articulates it as a “what if” speculation using a Machine Learning model trained with articles by authors that inspire our practice.
Our practice is heavily influenced by some key experiences: the former poetic innovation lab at CP+B; the work system from MESA; the approach to design and technology fostered at ITP/NYU. We are based in Amsterdam with projects in places like São Paulo, Boulder, Lisbon, Eindhoven, Maastricht, Turin, and Strasbourg.
